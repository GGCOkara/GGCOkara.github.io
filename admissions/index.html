Eligibility Criteria

Check if you qualify for our programs.

Intermediate (F.Sc/ICS/FA)

  • Matriculation (Science/Arts) from a recognized Board.
  • Pre-Medical: Minimum 60% Marks in Matric (Biology).
  • Pre-Engineering: Minimum 60% Marks in Matric.
  • ICS: Minimum 55% Marks in Matric.
  • Age limit: Maximum 18 years.
  • Domicile: Okara District preferred (Open Merit available).

BS Programs (4-Years)

  • Intermediate (FA/F.Sc/ICS) or equivalent (12 Years Education).
  • Minimum 45% Marks in Intermediate.
  • Subject relevancy required (e.g., Math marks for BS Math).
  • Entry Test: Not required (Merit based on Inter Marks).
  • Age limit: Maximum 24 years.
  • Both Male & Female candidates can apply (for specific programs).

How to Apply

1
Get Prospectus

Obtain the prospectus and admission form from the college admin block.

2
Fill Form

Fill the form carefully and attach attested copies of all required documents.

3
Submit

Submit the file to the relevant department committee for verification.

4
Pay Fee

Once merit list is displayed, collect challan and pay fee in BOP.

For Intermediate admissions, you must apply online via the Punjab OCAS Portal: ocas.punjab.gov.pk

Required Documents

  • 3 Attested copies of Matric/Inter Result Card.
  • 3 Attested copies of Character Certificate.
  • 3 Copies of CNIC / B-Form.
  • 3 Copies of Father/Guardian CNIC.
  • 4 Recent Passport size photographs (Blue bg).
  • Original NOC (if Board is other than Sahiwal).
