About Our College

A Legacy of Knowledge

The foundation stone of this roomy red-coloured building was laid on August 30, 1958. Situated in the heart of Okara, this imperial building is sheltered by indigenous neem and banyan trees, providing a serene environment for learning.

Starting as M.B High School in 1910, it was upgraded to a College in 1958 to fulfill the cherished desire of the masses. Today, it stands as a badge of dignity for the district.
